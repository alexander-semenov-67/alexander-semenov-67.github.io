I am a PhD student in mathematics. Welcome to my web page!

I work at the IRMA, in the team Modelization and control.

The title of my PhD thesis is: Dynamics of breathers of some dispersive equations. I do it under the supervision of Raphaël Côte.

The area of my works is the analysis of partial differential equations.

Here are some subjects in which I am interested and I work on:

  • modified Korteweg-de Vries equation,
  • solitons, breathers,
  • existence, uniqueness, regularity of multi-breathers,
  • orbital, asymptotic stability,
  • Liouville theorem at a neigbourhood of a soliton or a breather.
